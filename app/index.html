Brittani Manock

Something

Brittani has been involved and worked in the beauty & entertainment industries for over 20 years. She started doing make-up in thearter in middle school which ignitted her path to a career as a professional make-up artist. She attended JOE BLASCO SCHOOL for Make-Up Artistry in Orlando in 2005.

In 2013, She began training at HOLLYWOOD INSTUTE of BEAUTY CARRES for a license in Skin Care. After completing the Skin Care Program she extended her education by also earning a total of 3 Licenses which include COSMETOLOGY, SKIN CARE, & NAIL SPECIALIST.

Brittani has been working as a professional stylist at SALON RETRO since 2015 and has since become the owner with Maggie Byrnes.

Maggie Byrnes

Maggie's unique background living around the world has provided her a cultured and outpouring creative spirit. With formal fine arts education combined with cosmetology training from PAUL MITCHELL the school Orlando, she enjoys transforming a canvas and showing her clients the limitles possibilities of hair. Whether you need a complex UP-DO with BRAIDS and CURLS or a SLEEK, CLASSICE CUT and COLOR with a POLISHED FINISH, Maggie can't wait to help you make your style just that, completely yours!

Patti Migliorato Rieder
