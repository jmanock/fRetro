Brittani Manock 321.356.3557

Brittani has been involved and worked in the beauty and entertainment industries for over 20 years. She started doing make-up in theater in middle school which ignitted her path to a career as a professional Make-Up Artist. She attended Joe Blasco School for Make-Up Artistry in Orlando in 2005.

In 2013, she began training at Hollywood Instute of Beauty Careers for a license in Skin Car. After completing the Skin Care program she extended her education by also earning a total of 3 licenses which include:

  • Cosmetology
  • Skin Care
  • Nail Specialist

Brittani has been working as a professional stylist at Salon Retro since 2015 and has since become the owner.
