About

History

Salon Retro grand opening was January 7th, 2014 by Patti Migliorato Rieder who has been a stylist in Florida since 1976. She has sold Salon Retro June 2017 to Brittani Fernandez and Maggie Byrnes but is continuing to work at Salon Retro as a mentor and trainer for the new owners.

Our Team

Brittani Fernandez

321.356.3557

Brittani has been involved and worked in the beauty and entertainment industries for over 20 years. She started doing make-up in theater in middle school which ignitted her path to a career as a professional make-up artist. She attended Joe Blasco School For Make-Up Artistry in Orlando in 2005.

In 2013, She began training at Hollywood Instute of Beauty Carres for a license in Skin Care. After completing the Skin Care Program she extended her education by also earning a total of 3 Licenses which include Cosmetology, Skin Care, and Nail Specialist

Brittani has been working as a professional stylist at Salon Retro since 2015 and has since become the Salon owner with partner Maggie Byrnes

Maggie Byrnes

850.890.6922

Maggie's unique background living around the world has provided her a cultured and outpouring creative spirit. With formal fine arts education combined with cosmetology training from Paul Mitchell the school Orlando, she enjoys transforming a canvas and showing her clients the limitless possibilities of hair. Whether you need a complex up-do with braids and curls or a sleek, classic cut and color with a polished finish, Maggie can't wait to help you make your style just that, completely yours!

Patti Migliorato Rieder

Patti has been a stylist in Florida since 1976. A winner of over 250 awards in State, National and International compatitions in Hair Styling, Cutting, and Coloring. A winner of #1 Colorist in the USA in 1990 at Hair Color USA in Miami, Florida.
