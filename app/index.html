About

Salon Retro's grand opening was January 7th, 2014 by Patti Migliorato Rieder who has been a stylest in Florida since 1976. She has since sold Salon Retro to Brittani Fernandez but is continuing to work as a mentor and trainer for the new owner.

Brittani Manock

Brittani has been involved and worked in the beauty and entertainment industries for over 20 years. She started doing make-up in theater in middle school which ignitted her path to a career as a professional make-up artist. She attended Joe Blasco School for Make-Up Artistry in Orlando in 2005.

In 2013, she began training at Hollywood Instute of Beauty Carres for a license in Skin Care. After completing the Skin Care program she extended her education by also earning a total of 3 licenses which include:

  • Cosmetology
  • Skin Care
  • Nail Specialist

Brittani has been working as a professional stylist at Salon Retro since 2015 and has since become the owner.

Patti Migliorato Rieder

Patti has been a stylist in Florida since 1976. A winner of over 250 awards in State, National and International compatitions in:

  • Hair Styling
  • Cutting
  • Coloring
A winner of #1 Colorist in the USA in 1990 at Hair Color Usa in Miami, Florida.
